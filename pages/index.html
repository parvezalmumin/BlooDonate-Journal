Blood Donation For the first time

Date: 29 January, 2024

Place: Gazipur Adhunik Hospital & Diagnostic Centre

Experience: The feeling of donating blood for the first time was truly different. When I went to donate blood, I did not tell anyone at home because my mother is very afraid of needles. In fact, she was so scared that she did not even want to check her own blood group. Looking back, I probably should have informed my family before going, although, Alhamdulillah, everything went smoothly and there were no problems. After returning home, I messaged my sister and told her that I had donated blood that day and asked her not to tell anyone at home. Unfortunately, she ended up telling them anyway. I first learned about the blood donation request through a Messenger group. The person who shared the information was a friend of someone from our area and actually lived in our building. After I donated blood, he insisted on giving me some money for snacks. I did not want to accept it, but he was so persistent that I could not refuse. I felt quite embarrassed. The patient was undergoing some kind of surgery. I sincerely hope that he is doing well now.
